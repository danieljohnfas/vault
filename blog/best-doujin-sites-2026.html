What is the difference between doujin and doujinshi?

"Doujin" (同人) is a broad Japanese term referring to any self-published or fan-created work — including music, games, and manga. "Doujinshi" (同人誌) specifically refers to self-published manga or comics, often fan-made derivatives of popular anime and game characters. In the West, both terms are commonly used interchangeably to refer to fan-made manga and hentai comics.
