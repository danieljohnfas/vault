Copyright Policy

HentaiVault respects the intellectual property rights of others and complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If you believe a listing in our directory links to content that infringes your copyright, you may submit a takedown notice and we will remove the listing promptly.

What We Can Action

We can remove directory listings from our index. We cannot remove content from third-party websites — for that, you must contact the hosting site directly.

To Submit a DMCA Notice

Your notice must include the following:

  1. Your name and contact information (email address)
  2. A description of the copyrighted work you claim is being infringed
  3. The specific URL of the listing on HentaiVault that you want removed
  4. A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  5. A statement that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf
  6. Your electronic or physical signature

Where to Send

Submit DMCA notices via the Submit form on the homepage, selecting "DMCA" as the subject, or contact us directly via email. We aim to process all valid notices within 72 hours.

Counter-Notices

If you believe a listing was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notice. We will reinstate the listing within 10 business days unless the original complainant files a court action.
